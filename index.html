Once there was a tree... and she loved a little boy. And everyday the boy would come and he would gather her leaves and make them into crowns and play king of the forest. He would climb up her trunk and swing from her branches and eat apples. And they would play hide-and-go-seek. And when he was tired, he would sleep in her shade. And the boy loved the tree... very much. And the tree whas happy. But time went by. And the boy grew older. And the tree was often alone. Then one day the boy came to the tree and the tree said,
Ven, nino, subete a mi trono y mecete en mis ramas y come mis manzanas y juega bajo mi sombra y se feliz.
Come, Boy, come and climb up my trunk and swing from my branches and eat apples and play in my shade and be happy.
"I am too big to climb and play" said the boy. "I want to buy things and have fun. I want some money. Can you give me some money?"
"Lo siento" dijo el arbol, pero yo no tengo dinero. Solo tengo hojas y manzanas. Coge mis manzanas y vendelas en la cuidad. Asi tendras dinero y seras feliz.
"Im sorry," said the tree, "but I have no money. I have only leaves and apples. Take my apples, Boy and sell them in the city. Then you will have moeny and you will be happy."
And so the boy climbed up the tree and gathered her apples and carried them away. And the tree was happy. But the boy stayed away for a long time... and the tree was sad. and the one day the boy came back and the tree shook with joy and she said,
Ven, Nino, subete a mi tronco, mecete en mis ramas y se feliz.
Come, Boy climb up my trunk and swing from my branches and be happy.
"I am too busy to climb trees," said the boy. "I want a house to keep me warm," he said. "I want a wife and I want children, and so I need a house. Can you give me a house?"
"Yo no tengo casa", dijo el arbol, "El bosque es mi hogar, pero tu puedes cortar mis ramas y hacerte una casa. Entonces seras feliz".
"The forest is my house, but you may cut off my brances and and build a house. Then you will be happy."
And so the boy cut off her branches and carried them away to build his house.
And the tree was happy
But the boy stayed away for a long time. And when he came back, the tree was so happy she could hardly speak.
"Ven, Nino" susurro. "Ven y juega".
"Come, Boy," she whispered, "come and play."
"I am too old and sad to play," said the boy."I want a boat that will take me far away from here. Can you give me a boat?"
"Corta mi tronco y hazte un bote", dijo el arbol. "Enotnces podras navegar lejos... y seras feliz."
"Cut down my trunk and make a boat, said the tree then you can sail away... and be happy."
And so the boy cut down her trunk and made a boat and sailed away. And the tree was happy... but not really.
And after a long time the boy came back again.
"Lo siento, Nino," dijo el arbol, "pero ya no tengo nada para darte-- Ya no me quedan manzanas".
"I am sorry, Boy," said the tree, "but I have nothing left to give you-- My apples are gone".
"My teeth are too weak for apples," said the boy.
"Ya no me quedan ramas" dijo el arbol. "Tu ya no puedes mecerte en ellas--"
"My branches are gone," said the tree. "You cannot swing on them--"
"I am too old to swing on branches," said the boy.
"My trunk is gone," said tree. "You cannot climb--"
"Ya no tengo tonco" dijo el arbol. "Tu ya no puedes trepar--"
"I am too tired to climb," said the boy.
"Lo siento" se lamento el arbol "Quisiera poder darte algo... pero ya no me queda nada. Soy solo un viejo tocon. Lo siento..."
"I am sorry" sighed the tree. "I wish that I could give you something... but I have nothing left. I am just an old stump. I am sorry..."
"I don't need very much now," said the boy, "just a quiet place to sit and rest. I am very tired. "
"Bien", dijo el arbol reanimandose, "un viejo tocon es bueno para sentarse y descansar. Ven, Nino, sientate. Sientate y descansa."
"Well, an old stump is good for sitting and resting. Come, Boy, sit down. Sit down and rest."
And the boy did.
